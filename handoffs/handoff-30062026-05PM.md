## Big Decisions Made

### Product, Campaign, and Item relationship

We clarified that `FlashSaleCampaign` should not directly own products.

Correct model:

```text
FlashSaleCampaign 1 - N FlashSaleItem N - 1 Product
```

Meaning:

- `Product` is the base catalog product.
- `FlashSaleCampaign` is the sale event, for example 7.7, 8.8, Black Friday.
- `FlashSaleItem` connects a product to a campaign and stores sale-specific data like sale price and stock.

This lets the same product appear in multiple campaigns with different sale prices and quantities.
